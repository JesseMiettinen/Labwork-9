Welcome to Treat yourself!

You can use this app to make yourself a wishlist of of things you want. Treats can be anything you want, electronics, snacks or a dinner in a fancy restaurant!

How to use this app?

After logging on please start by inserting the date of when you are allowed to treat yourself next time, this is down to your own consideration.

What then?

After setting the date of your next treat. On the next page you can add treats, change their order by dragging them and delete treats by swiping.

Treat yourself!

Keep the treats you want the most on top of the list! Using this app will help you to either control your spending or your calorie intake depending of the user.
